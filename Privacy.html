Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09-03-2025

Welcome to SRT Creator ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website ([Your Website URL]).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or SRT file content to our servers. All data remains on your local browser storage and is never sent to us or third parties.

However, we use Google Analytics to collect non-personally identifiable information about website usage, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Browser type and device information
  • General location data (e.g., country/city)

This data helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since your SRT file data is stored locally on your browser, we do not access or use it in any way. The Google Analytics data we collect is used solely for:

  • Monitoring website performance
  • Understanding user behavior
  • Improving our services

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Google Analytics may use cookies to track website usage. These cookies collect anonymized data and do not identify you personally. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share any user data with third parties. However, Google Analytics, a third-party service, may collect and process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Your SRT files and related data never leave your device. As such, we do not store any personal data that could be compromised.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can clear your browser storage at any time to remove all saved SRT data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
